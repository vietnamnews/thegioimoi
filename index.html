UCO COVID-19 protocols remain in place for the spring 2021 semester. Masks are required on campus when around others. Face Mask Tips and FAQ

At UCO, wearing a mask and engaging in physical distancing are not just the rules; it's the right thing to do. These practices have been shown to slow the spread of COVID-19 and save lives. We encourage all Bronchos to educate those around you to team up to do the right thing.

COVID-19 Vaccine Reporting

UCO faculty, staff and students who have received the COVID-19 vaccine are encouraged to self-report their vaccination by filling out the voluntary vaccination survey.

UCO Plans In-Person Classes for Fall 2021

UCO plans to hold classes in-person for the fall 2021 semester while still adhering to Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC) guidelines and encourages all students, faculty and staff to get the COVID-19 vaccination, when eligible.
